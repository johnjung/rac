Rights and Reproductions

Citing Images

All images in the Rose & Chess collection may be used for educational and scholarly purposes, but any such use requires that a credit line be included with each image used.

Le Roman de la Rose

Credit Line: Le Roman de la Rose, University of Chicago Library MS 1380, [image number], [leaf number], Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center, University of Chicago Library. Image courtesy of The Sheridan Libraries, Johns Hopkins University.

e.g. the title page would be cited as:
Le Roman de la Rose, University of Chicago Library MS 1380, image 13, leaf 1r, Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center, University of Chicago Library. Image courtesy of The Sheridan Libraries, Johns Hopkins University.

Le Jeu des échecs moralisé

Credit Line: Le Jeu des échecs moralisé, University of Chicago Library MS 392, [image number], [leaf number], Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center, University of Chicago Library.

e.g. the title page would be cited as: Le Jeu des échecs moralisé, University of Chicago Library MS 392, image 13, leaf 1r, Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center, University of Chicago Library.

Requesting Permission and Reproductions

Use of these materials for publication in any medium requires permission. Use of materials for commercial publication projects may also be subject to a use fee.

Le Roman de la Rose

Requesting permission: Use of images from Le Roman de la Rose requires permission from The University of Chicago and Johns Hopkins University. Please consult the permissions policies of the Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center and the Sheridan Library's Roman de la Rose site.

Requesting reproductions: To request high resolution reproductions of images from Le Roman de la Rose please see the Sheridan Library's Roman de la Rose site.

Le Jeu des échecs moralisé

Requesting permission: Use of images from Le Jeu des échecs moralisé requires permission from The University of Chicago. Please consult the permissions policies of the Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center.

Requesting reproductions: To request high resolution reproductions of images from Le Jeu des échecs moralisé please consult the Hanna Holborn Gray Special Collections Research Center's Copying Services page.
